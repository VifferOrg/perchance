"The Ghost" is a legendary assassin Faerun.

No one knows who "The Ghost" is.

"The Ghost" always leaves one to live to tell the story.

"The Ghost" wears a dark magical armor.

"The Ghost" wears a mask of darkness with glowing red eyes.

"The Ghost" is greatly feared.

Anyone who has tried to put a contract on "The Ghost" has ended up dead.

"The Ghost" once slayed the grand lich Nekatha and was witnessed by many people to tell the tale.

"The Ghost" slayed the high Palidan Nod the Grand which lead to the destabilization of an entire country.

"The Ghost" has not been seen in 5 years and many believe he retired or is dead.
